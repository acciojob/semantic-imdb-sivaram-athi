Summary

Inception is a science fiction film that explores the realm of shared dreams. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious. Cobb is offered a chance to have his past crimes forgiven, but he must plant an idea in a target's mind, a process called inception, which is considered to be impossible.
